FlipGame



GAME RULES

  • In this game you have to find the unique image among nine images.
  • you have three choices to make.
  • Initially you have 3 choices.For every click choices are decreased by 1.If you find the correct one then you will win the game
  • Here you have 20s of time to make choices.If you take more than 20s then it will give Time's up!
